About Me

Once I was Peter Pan.

I flew around the stage and I thought I was on top of the world, and I was able to tell a timeless story. However, I really was just attatched to a cable that flew me around on the stage. But for the kids watching, it was pure magic, and that is was made it worth it to me.

That is how I can describe my journey with coding. Computers are magic, and even if not everyone knows exactly how it works, It still is amazing that I can take a small part to make that happen. I live in Utah, have always loved it, and I am happily married to my wife Justine.
